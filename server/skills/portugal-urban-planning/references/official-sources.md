# Official Sources

Use these as the initial official source registry for the Portugal baseline:

| Source | Type | Why it matters |
|--------|------|----------------|
| `Decreto-Lei n.º 555/99, de 16 de dezembro (RJUE)` | National legal framework | Core regime for urbanization and building procedures in Portugal. |
| `Diário da República` publication of municipality regulations | Official municipal publication | Confirms the regulation text in force and its publication history. |
| Municipality website pages for urbanism / licensing | Official municipality operational source | Practical process, forms, contacts, and municipality-specific implementation details. |
| Municipality fee table | Official municipality fee source | Needed for procedural cost expectations and fee references. |

## Working method

- Start from the official publication when the question is legal or procedural.
- Use municipality website pages for operational details, forms, and local implementation.
- Use the fee table only for fee references; do not extrapolate undocumented values.
