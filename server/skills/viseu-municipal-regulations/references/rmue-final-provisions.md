# RMUE Final Provisions

Primary source:
- `Aviso n.o 12538/2020`, arts. `84-88`
- PDF: https://files.dre.pt/2s/2020/08/167000000/0033500372.pdf

Use this file for public-administration procedures, municipal offences, conflict resolution, and the formal entry-into-force rules.

## Article 84 - urban operations promoted by Public Administration

- For the purpose of the RJUE article 7(2) opinion, urban operations promoted by Public Administration must have the same instruction as operations promoted by private parties.
- Promoting entities must deliver to the municipality:
  - one paper copy of all specialty projects
  - one `CD`

Agent use:
- this is relevant when the applicant/promoter is a public entity; do not assume a lighter documentary burden.

## Article 85 - administrative offences (`contraordenacoes`)

RMUE expressly sanctions at least these situations:
- execution of ramps in the sidewalk zone
- alteration of municipal conditions on public-space occupation or tapumes/vedacoes, except where the change reduces occupation in favor of circulation
- fencing, occupying, blocking access to public space, or intervening in public-road pavement without prior control
- failure to communicate the start of urban works up to `5 days` before they start

Penalty band stated in RMUE:
- fine between `EUR 75` and `EUR 750`
- without prejudice to restoration of the preexisting situation where applicable

Guardrail:
- RMUE offence rules should not be treated as an exhaustive offence catalogue for all RJUE breaches.

## Article 86 - conflict resolution

- Interested parties may request intervention of an arbitral commission under RJUE article 118 for conflict resolution in the application of the RMUE.

## Article 87 - entry into force

- The RMUE enters into force `15 days` after its publication in the 2nd series of Diario da Republica.

## Article 88 - revocation rule

- Municipal regulatory provisions contrary to this RMUE are revoked.
- Exception: fee provisions from the prior `RMUET` continue to apply until that regime is revised.

## Agent use notes

- Use article 85 sparingly and only when the fact pattern really matches the municipal offence description.
- Article 88 matters when the user is comparing old municipality rules against the RMUE in force.
