# Urbanism Entry Points

Operational source categories to verify before answering process questions:

| Category | Official source |
|----------|-----------------|
| Urbanism / licensing landing pages | Município de Viseu website (`cm-viseu.pt`) |
| Public consultation / regulation PDFs | Município de Viseu participation pages |
| Official regulation publication | Diário da República (`dre.pt`) |

## Working rule

- For process questions, prefer municipality website pages.
- For legal wording, prefer Diário da República or municipality-published regulation PDFs.
- If the municipality site and the publication differ, flag the difference and prefer the published act for legal wording.
