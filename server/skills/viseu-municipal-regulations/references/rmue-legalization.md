# RMUE Legalization And Final Technical Deliverables

Primary source:
- `Aviso n.o 12538/2020`, arts. `80-83`
- PDF: https://files.dre.pt/2s/2020/08/167000000/0033500372.pdf

Use this file for existing unlicensed buildings, information requests about legalization, execution-specialty projects, and final drawings.

## Article 80 - legalization procedure for urban operations

### Trigger

- For completed buildings without prior-control procedure and without authorization of use, the interested party must initiate legalization.
- If they do not, municipality may notify them to do so within a period not shorter than `10 days` and not longer than `90 days`.

### When no corrective works are needed

- Legalization is titled by `alvara de autorizacao de utilizacao`.
- That title must expressly mention that the building was legalized.

### When corrective or adaptation works are needed

- The interested party must prepare the relevant projects.
- Execution of those works is titled by an `alvara de obras de edificacao`.
- After those works, a request for authorization of use follows under the legal regime.

### Municipal inspection

- Legalization is normally preceded by a municipal inspection (`vistoria municipal`).
- Notification of the inspection must be sent at least `8 days` in advance.
- Inspection checks:
  - general conservation state
  - urban insertion
  - objective validation of the evidence submitted
- The inspection report must state:
  - property identification
  - its condition
  - need for correction or adaptation works, if any
  - answers to owner questions raised in the procedure

### Cases where inspection may be dispensed

- Prior inspection may be dispensed if the request does not seek any dispensation from the technical/instruction elements required by RJUE and the applicable instruction ordinance.

### Deadlines and titles

- Authorization of use must be requested within `30 business days` after legalization approval.
- The authorization title must explicitly state that the building was subject to legalization.

## Article 81 - information request on how legalization should proceed

An information request about legalization terms must include:
- title/right documents authorizing the operation
- extracts from applicable municipal plans and constraints
- location and framing plan
- georeferenced implantation plan
- architectural survey of the existing building
- descriptive report including the applicant questions
- photographic survey of the building and surroundings

## Article 82 - execution project

- The promoter must submit copies of specialty execution projects within `60 days` from the start of works, or at the start of the procedure if preferred.
- Content is the responsibility of the authors and must match operation complexity.
- Rule-of-thumb scale: `1/50`, or another justified scale.

## Article 83 - telas finais

- Municipality should require final drawings of architecture and specialties whenever changes occurred during execution.

## Agent use notes

- Distinguish clearly between:
  - legalization with no works
  - legalization with corrective or adaptation works
  - preliminary information on how legalization should proceed
- For legalization checklists, pair this file with municipality operational guidance and any current NIPs.
